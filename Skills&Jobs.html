Skills and Jobs

The 4 key roles required for GamR are and operations officer, front end developer, Programmer and cloud engineer. Being a small start up with a clear direction of where we are heading initially, we don’t need any roles like a mature company has and these include the likes of business development and strategic analysis. These roles in mature companies exist as they have peaked with their first idea and need to expand but GamR is still in its infancy.

Below is a Job Description for each position.

Operations Officer

Position title: Operations Officer
Position Number: 001001
Classification: Senior Manager level 4
Reports to: CEO/ CTO
Special conditions: N/A

Position summary

Your primary role is to be responsible and accountable for the daily tasking of machinery and personnel, which includes ensuring jobs are performed safely, accurately and in accordance with company and customer requirements.

People management

  • Ensure a safe working environment for all employees.
  • Lead a team successfully in all aspects of the project including guidance, support and development.
  • Provide induction training to any new members of the team.
  • Provide leadership and guidance.
  • Lead and mentor staff.
  • Provide feedback and performance management of staff.
  • Monitor and improve processes as required.
  • Ensure compliancy.

Responsibilities

  • Administrative oversight of all business operations, planning, development and business direction. This role will be the one that connects everyone together and gets them working as a group.
  • This role in Gamr will create operational procedures, manage the team, innovative and manipulate operation strategies and manage company resources.

Requirements

  • Experience in people management.
  • Understanding of application KPI's, mobile app development, product costings etc.

Front End Designer

Position title: Front end designer
Position Number: 001002
Classification: Bachelor degree
Reports to: Operations engineer
Special conditions: Work from office or home

Position summary

The primary role of this position is to participate in the specification, design, development, review and testing of mobile applications, web portals and other applications’ Front-End.

People management

  • Work with Front End team to deliver required outcomes.

Responsibilities

  • Design the graphical elements/assets for the applications and websites.
  • This role in Gamr will to create assets for the mobile application that may have versions created for the web version. This role will also be to code the CSS HMTL for the website.

Requirements

  • Demonstrable knowledge of front-end web development using HTML, CSS and JavaScript (jQuery).
  • A willingness to improve your skills and keep up to date with technological advancements.

Back End Programmer

Position title: Back end programmer
Position Number: 001003
Classification: Bachelor degree
Reports to: Senior security engineer
Special conditions: Work from office or home

Position summary

You will be a software developer who enjoys the challenge of designing and delivering bespoke solutions to complex problems. You will work with our cross functional teams of business analysts, experience designers, product managers and data engineers on site with clients. You will work all the way from discovery to delivery and beyond to solve real business problems.

People management

  • Work with Back End team to develop solutions to difficult problems.

Responsibilities

  • Code the application with direction from the operations officer and use assist designed by the front-end developer.
  • This role in Gamr will create the mobile application for mobile phones to communicate with the cloud servers and coding on those servers.

Requirements

  • Good understanding of frontend development models and frameworks (HTML/CSS/Javascript/AngularJS).
  • Strong and proven computer science or software engineering background
  • Cryptography and public key infrastructure

Cloud Engineer

Position title: Cloud engineer
Position Number: 001004
Classification: Bachelor degree
Reports to: Senior cloud engineer
Special conditions: N/A

Position summary

As a Cloud Engineer, you will focus on supporting managing and enhancing high-quality IaaS and Cloud-native services used by staff, customers and partners. You will ensure the availability and resilience of the AWS and Azure environment while providing best-practice advice, support and recommendations for supported IT business infrastructure, systems and services.

People management

  • N/A

Responsibilities

  • Host the company’s core product in a cloud environment while also maintaining the internal applications like 365.
  • This role in Gamr will to manage the servers and services that the programmer has written code for. The Cloud engineer will work closely with the programmer as well with all employees in a technical support role for internal services running on the cloud.

Requirements

  • Experience managing and supporting a mix of on-premise, AWS platform workloads and associated systems to agreed Service Levels.
  • Experience supporting and building AWS cloud hosted platforms.
  • Experience with server patch management processes and tools.
