Group Assignment

We have been tasked with the following objectives for this assignment:

Group Website on GitHub:

This website itself is part of our assignment, to present to you our team and assignment findings, via the magic of leprechauns and the world wide web, using GitHub pages.

Link Personal Profiles:

Our previous assignment was to create a personal profile using GitHub Pages, which are summarised and linked here in this assignment.

Ideal job:

To research our ideal jobs and interview a successful person from one of those fields.

Industry Skills:

To research the industries & skills connected to such career aspirations.

Technology Research:

Research associated technology and its likely impact on ourselves, career, and the world at large.

Group Project:

Research & plan a hypothetical group project relating to some use/application of Information Technology.

Assignment Analysis:

To analyse the tools and methods we have used in this assignment, our efforts, and results.
